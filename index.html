How It Works

What is Overdrive?

Overdrive is a strategy-first racing board game. Choose your car, build your mod deck, and master three unique tracks to win the Overdrive Cup. It's part deck-builder, part tactical battler, part gearhead fantasy.

Pick Your Ride

Upgrade Everything

Buy and install mods that affect your car’s handling, top speed, durability, or endurance. Tune your car to match the demands of each track. Every mod matters.

How It Plays

  • 🧩 Draft your car and begin with a base mod deck
  • 🛠 Win credits through performance and events
  • 🏁 Upgrade between races at the Mod Shop
  • 🏆 Score points over 3 races — most points wins
