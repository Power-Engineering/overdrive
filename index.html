What is Overdrive?

Overdrive is a customizable racing board game with real-world cars, track strategy, and mod upgrades. Compete across three iconic tracks in a persistent race weekend format.

Core Features

  • 🏎️ Deck-building meets racing
  • ⚙️ Real car stats & tuning upgrades
  • 🎮 Dynamic events, cornering tactics, wear management
  • 🛠️ Mod shop between races
