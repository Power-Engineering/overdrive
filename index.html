How It Works

What is Overdrive?

Overdrive is a strategy deck-building street racing game. Choose real cars, mod them with performance upgrades, and outmaneuver rivals across dynamic race tracks. Intense. Customizable. Always replayable.

Pick Your Ride

Upgrade Everything

Use credits to equip your car with turbo kits, tires, brakes, and suspension upgrades. Your mods shape your race strategy. Build smart, drive fast.

How It Plays

  • Draft your car and mod deck
  • Race across three strategic tracks
  • Manage wear, weather, and rival sabotage
  • Score the highest across the circuit to win
