What is Overdrive?

Overdrive is a high-strategy racing board game where players pick real-world inspired cars, equip mods, and race across unique tracks.

Choose Your Ride

Mod Your Car

Equip your vehicle with cold air intakes, forced induction, sport suspension, and more. Some upgrades only work with certain engine types!

How It Plays

  • Draft your car from a deck of real-world inspired vehicles
  • Earn currency and upgrade between races
  • Face random events, terrain changes, and head-to-head battles
